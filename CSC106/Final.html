Final Program

This program was designed by Greg Piturro to predict what a persons first move will most likely be in a game of rock, paper, scissors. In order to start the quiz, press the start button. From there, there'll be a series of 10 questions that will determine a persons initial choice in rock, paper, scissors based on their personality via responses to each question. In order to answer a question, press the buttons that appear below the question presented. After 10 questions, you'll receive your results!
